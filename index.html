The Assassin Site easily eliminates the difficulties of organizing a game of assassin, whether it's across a student body or just among your friends.

What is Assassin?

Senior assassin is a fun end of the year tradition where seniors try to squirt each other with water guns.

Rules

These are the genral rules. Rules may vary from game to game.
  1. You use squirt guns or nerf guns
  2. You can't play at school

Services

We will organize you game for you and keep track of the targets and assassins. All you have to do is sign up and hit "create a new game".
