Create. Organize. Play

The Assassin Site easily eliminates the difficulties of organizing a game of assassin, whether it's across a student body or just among your friends.

What is Assassin?

Assassin is a popular group game found in schools, camps, and workplaces around the world. With our website, it's easier than ever to organize as many games as you want, with as many players as you want.

Create a Game

With a single free account on our website, you can create and run as many games as you like. There are no limits - no caps on players, no restrictions on time. Just sign in or sign up to create your own game today.

Join a Game

Just looking to play? No problem. With a free account, you can join any number of games. Just use our search functionalities or get an invite link from your friends to get started.
